Bees

Bees are vital pollinators that play a crucial role in maintaining
ecosystems and supporting global. food production. These industrious
insects travel from flower to flower, collecting nectar and pollen,
which they use to produce honey and feed their colonies. In the process,
they facilitate the pollination of many plants, including fruits, vegetables
, and nuts, making them essential for biodiversity and agriculture. Bees live
in well-organized colonies led by a queen, supported by worker bees, and a few
drones. However, bee populations are declining due to threats like habitat loss
, pesticides, climate change, and diseases, highlighting the urgent need for
conservation efforts to protect these remarkable creatures
